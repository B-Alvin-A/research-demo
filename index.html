About us

Grounded in
evidence.

ApexResearch is an independent consultancy combining social science methodology with deep sector expertise across public health, development, and private markets.

01

Primary research

Surveys, interviews, focus groups, and ethnographic fieldwork designed with statistical rigour and adapted to local contexts.

02

Data analysis

Quantitative and qualitative analysis to surface patterns, test hypotheses, and translate raw data into clear findings.

03

Policy evaluation

Independent assessment of programme effectiveness, outcomes measurement, and value-for-money analysis for funders and implementers.

04

Strategic advisory

Translating research findings into clear, actionable recommendations that leadership teams can act on with confidence.
